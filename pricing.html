Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

General

What is Cycls?

Cycls is a software platform that enables developers and businesses to design, publish, share, and monetize AI-powered applications. It offers a unique approach to creating user interfaces for AI apps, combining an intuitive SDK for automated UI generation with a robust publishing platform. This allows for seamless integration of app logic and data management with user-friendly interfaces.

What is the Superchat?

Superchat is a key feature of Cycls, providing a unified chat interface that integrates various AI apps built on Sarya. It allows users to interact with multiple AI applications through a single, streamlined chat platform, enhancing user experience and engagement. For developers, Superchat offers a versatile way to present and connect their AI apps to users, fostering an interactive and dynamic app ecosystem.

Who is Cycls for?

Cycls is designed for innovators and creators in the AI space, from individual developers and tech startups to large enterprises. Its user-friendly interface and automated UI generation make it ideal for those looking to integrate AI into their applications efficiently. Sarya is also perfect for businesses aiming to leverage AI for customer engagement and revenue generation, offering advanced tools for analytics and monetization. Whether it's for exploring AI capabilities, scaling up operations, or enterprise-level deployment, Sarya is tailored to meet diverse development needs.

Plans

Can I start using Cycls for free?

Yes, you can start with our Hacker plan at no cost. It includes unlimited deployments, basic analytics, and public support, perfect for individuals and small teams starting out.

What do you mean by "Unlimited deployments"?

Unlimited deployments" means that you can launch an unlimited number of different AI apps on our platform. There's no limit to the quantity of apps you can deploy, allowing you the freedom to explore, create, and publish as many AI applications as your projects require.

What additional features does the Growth plan offer?

The Growth plan offers private Superchat with your branding and domain, advanced analytics for deeper insights, and a 24-hour support response time, ideal for growing businesses.

Can the Enterprise plan be customized to fit our specific business needs?

Absolutely. The Enterprise plan is designed for customization to meet the unique requirements of your business. Feel free to contact us and we will be happy to support you.

What are my payment options?

Cycls accepts all major credit cards for our Growth and Enterprise plans.

How will I be billed?

The Growth plan operates on an annual subscription model, conveniently billed on a monthly basis. As for the Enterprise plan, it offers a customized billing structure tailored to meet your unique business needs. For specific details about the Enterprise plan's billing arrangements, please get in touch with our sales team.

Technical

Does Cycls host my AI app?

Cycls exclusively hosts the user interface of your AI app. The backend, encompassing all the code you develop, remains under your control and can be deployed to any infrastructure of your choice.

This setup ensures that while Cycls efficiently manages the front-end presentation, you retain full autonomy over your app's backend deployment and data management.

What services does Cycls integrate with?

Because Cycls SDK runs in your codebase, Cycls SDK is compatible with whatever services you use in your backend.

What languages does Cycls support?

Currently the Cycls SDK supports Python.

What language models does Cycls support?

Cycls offers flexible support for a variety of language models, including OpenAI's advanced models and various open-source alternatives. This flexibility allows you to choose the language model that best fits your app's needs and objectives.

Support

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, Cycls provides technical support across all plans. The Hacker plan includes basic community support, the Growth plan offers a faster 24-hour response, and the Enterprise plan delivers dedicated support with a rapid 1-hour response time.
